pyDockDNA is a tool for the structural prediction of protein-DNA (and protein-protein) interactions.

Given the 3D coordinates of two interacting molecular partners (protein and DNA), pyDockDNA returns the best rigid-body docking orientations generated by FTDock ( Gabb HA. et al. J Mol Biol 1997;272:106-120.) and evaluated by pyDockDNA scoring function, which includes electrostatics, desolvation energy and limited van der Waals contribution.
