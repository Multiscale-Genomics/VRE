pyDock is a tool for the structural prediction of protein-protein interactions.

Given the 3D coordinates of two interacting molecular partners (protein or protein complex), pyDock returns the best rigid-body docking orientations generated by FTDock (Gabb HA. et al. J Mol Biol 1997;272:106-120) and evaluated by pyDock scoring function, which includes electrostatics, desolvation energy and limited van der Waals contribution (Cheng TM. et al. Proteins 2007;68(2):503-15.).
