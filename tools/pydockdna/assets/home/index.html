pyDockDNA is a tool for the structural prediction of protein-DNA interactions.

Given the 3D coordinates of two interacting molecular partners (protein or DNA), pyDockDNA returns the best rigid-body docking orientations generated by FTDock (Gabb HA. et al. J Mol Biol 1997;272:106-120) and evaluated by pyDockDNA scoring function, which includes electrostatics energy and limited van der Waals contribution.
